CRO(Cathode Ray Oscilloscope )

*A cathode ray oscilloscope (CRO) is a device that converts electrical signals into an active graphical representation on a fluorescent screen.

*A cathode ray oscilloscope is a very fast X-Y plotter that can display an input signal versus time or another signal.

*Oscilloscopes (or scopes) test and display voltage signals as waveforms, visual representations of the variation of voltage over time.
