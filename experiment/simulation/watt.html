WATTMETER


*The wattmeter is an instrument for measuring the electric active power (or the average of the rate of flow of electrical energy) in watts of any given circuit.
*A wattmeter reads the average value of the product v(t)i(t) = p(t), where v(t) is the voltage.
*The working of induction type wattmeter is based on the principle of electromagnetic induction.
